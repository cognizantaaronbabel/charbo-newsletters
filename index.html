Message from the Principal

Dear parents and guardians,

It is with great pride that I announce that our school has successfully completed the evaluation for the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme.

This recognition confirms the quality of our educational approach, the dedication of our team, and the consistency of our practices with the standards required by the International Baccalaureate Organization.

This achievement reflects a collective effort: our staff, our students, and you — the parents — who support us every day. Thanks to this positive evaluation, our school continues its commitment to rigorous, inclusive, and globally-minded education.

We also want to announce that the school is preparing a competition involving its feeder schools (École élémentaire Sainte-Madeleine and École élémentaire Saint-Noël Chabanel). The competition will focus on robotics, technology, and animation as part of the prendsTech program — designed to introduce young people to specialized trades requiring computer programming skills. On April 16, students will present to the school community how they tackled these challenges.

On behalf of the school staff, I thank each of you for your trust and support. I wish you an excellent March Break.

Sincerely,
Eddy Wambo, Principal.

Spring Leadership Conference (HOSA)

Mar 9–11 HOSA Spring Leadership Conference — Metro Toronto Convention Centre +CAL

Nine Charbonnel students will attend the HOSA Spring Leadership Conference from March 9 to 11 at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre.

HOSA (Health Occupations Students of America) is the largest health sciences student organization in North America. Our students will participate in workshops and competitions focused on health and medicine. Winners may advance to a conference in Indianapolis.

More information: hosacanada.org/home
